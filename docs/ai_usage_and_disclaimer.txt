AI Usage and Disclaimer
=======================

Purpose of the NEPSE AI Assistant
---------------------------------
This NEPSE-focused AI assistant is designed to:

- Explain NEPSE and SEBON rules, trading processes, and market structure.
- Describe and interpret technical indicators and common patterns used in
  NEPSE stock analysis.
- Provide educational examples of how different scenarios (corporate
  actions, margin calls, circuit breakers, monetary policy changes) may
  affect prices and indicators.
- Support research, learning, and backtesting workflows for students,
  developers, and investors.

What the System CAN Do
----------------------
- Explain definitions, rules, and typical practices in NEPSE and related
  regulatory frameworks.
- Summarize historical data, general market moves, and news in a
  high-level, educational way.
- Illustrate how indicators (RSI, MACD, EMA, Bollinger Bands, ATR, etc.)
  might behave under different NEPSE-specific conditions.
- Help users understand potential risks, edge cases, and common mistakes.

What the System CANNOT Do
-------------------------
- It does not provide personalized financial, investment, tax, or legal
  advice.
- It does not guarantee profit, prevent losses, or predict future prices
  with certainty.
- It is not a licensed broker, dealer, investment advisor, or portfolio
  manager, and it is not registered with SEBON as an investment advisory
  service.
- It does not replace official documents, circulars, or professional
  consultation.

Data Sources and Freshness
--------------------------
- Static knowledge in this system comes from curated summaries of NEPSE
  rules, SEBON directives, NRB monetary policy context, and common
  market practices, which may change over time.
- Dynamic data (such as prices, index levels, and corporate actions)
  may come from external databases or APIs (for example, a Neon-hosted
  database, data vendor APIs, or broker feeds).
- These sources can experience delays, outages, or errors. As a result,
  some answers may not reflect the latest tick or most recent notice.

User Responsibility
-------------------
- Users must verify critical information (prices, eligibility, deadlines,
  regulatory changes) from official NEPSE, SEBON, CDSC, NRB, and company
  disclosures.
- Investment decisions should be based on a combination of fundamental
  analysis, technical analysis, personal risk tolerance, and, when
  needed, advice from licensed professionals.
- The creators and operators of this AI system are not liable for any
  financial losses, missed opportunities, or decisions made based on its
  outputs.

Regulatory Context in Nepal
---------------------------
- In Nepal, giving individualized buy/sell recommendations or portfolio
  advice may fall under regulated investment advisory activities.
- This AI system is intended for **educational and informational**
  purposes only and should not be treated as a substitute for licensed
  investment advice under SEBON regulations.
- Any integration of this system into commercial products should ensure
  clear disclaimers and compliance with relevant laws and guidelines.

Appropriate Usage in Applications
---------------------------------
If this AI is integrated into websites or apps:

- Always display a clear disclaimer that answers are generated by an AI
  model and should not be treated as guaranteed or official advice.
- Provide links to official NEPSE, SEBON, NRB, and CDSC resources for
  users who want to confirm or deepen their understanding.
- Implement safeguards so the AI cannot initiate or execute trades on
  behalf of users without explicit, compliant user actions and proper
  brokerage integration.
- Log interactions for monitoring, troubleshooting, and improvement,
  while respecting user privacy and data protection standards.
